How It Works



1. Register

Sign up as a farmer or dealer.

2. Browse & Connect

Find the best deals with advanced filtering.

Farmers can list their crops with complete details, including pricing, quantity, and quality certifications. Dealers can browse through verified listings using smart filters to find the best deals based on their needs, ensuring a smooth and transparent trading process.

CropDeal provides secure payment gateways to protect both buyers and sellers. Transactions are encrypted, ensuring that farmers get fair prices and dealers receive high-quality crops. With real-time tracking, users stay updated on orders from payment to delivery.
